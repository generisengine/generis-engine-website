SERVICES

What Generis Engine Offers

Scene Engine

Handles Creation and Management of world scenes.

Entity Engine

Handles Creation and Management of entities.

Event Engine

Handles Creation and Management of events.

Input Engine

Handles all world inputs. e.g Keyboard, Mouse, Touchscreen, ...

Render Engine

Handles everything related world rendering.

Sound Manager

Handles everything related world sounds.



Sprites Manager

Enables you to use Spritesheets.

Tiles Manager

Enables you to use Tile Maps.

Helper Functions

Functions to make your game dev life a lot more easier. e.g walk, jump, attack ...

Effects

Tween & Transition effects

Cross Browser Compatible

Compatible with all major browsers & mobile devices:

Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Opera, IE, Android, iOS

Cloud

Save and Publish your game in the cloud.
